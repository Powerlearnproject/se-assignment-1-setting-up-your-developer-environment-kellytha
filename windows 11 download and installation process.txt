Step 1: Download the Windows 11 Installation Assistant
Go to the Microsoft Windows 11 download page.
Under the "Windows 11 Installation Assistant" section, click on the "Download now" button.
Step 2: Run the Windows 11 Installation Assistant
Open the downloaded file (Windows11InstallationAssistant.exe).
Follow the on-screen instructions to check your PCâ€™s compatibility.
Once compatibility is confirmed, the assistant will guide you through the process of downloading and installing Windows 11.
Step 3: Accept License Terms
Read and accept the license terms by clicking "Accept and Install".

Step 4: Installation Process
The installation assistant will download Windows 11 files. This might take some time depending on your internet speed.
Once the download is complete, it will begin preparing the installation.
After preparation, it will prompt you to restart your PC. Click on the "Restart now" button.
Step 5: Post-Restart Installation
Your PC will restart several times during the installation process.
Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup. This includes settings like region, keyboard layout, and connecting to a network.
Log in with your Microsoft account or create a new one if prompted.
Step 6: Complete Setup
After logging in, follow any additional setup steps.
Your desktop will appear, and Windows 11 will be installed.
